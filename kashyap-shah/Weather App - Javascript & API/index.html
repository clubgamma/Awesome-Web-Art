Conversion Formula
Celcius To Fahrenheit (0°C × 9/5) + 32 = 32°F
Celcius To Kelvin 0°C + 273.15 = 273.15K
Fahrenheit To Celcius (0°F - 32) × 5/9 = -17.78°C
Fahrenheit To Kelvin (0°F - 32) × 5/9 + 273.15 = 255.372 K
Kelvin To Celcius 0 K - 273.15 = -273.1°C
Kelvin To Fahrenheit (0 K - 273.15) × 9/5 + 32 = -459.7°F
